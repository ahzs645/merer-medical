Table 6 - Harmonised reference intervals for chemical pathology

RCPA Standardardised Pathology Informatics in Australia (SPIA) Chemical Pathology Harmonised Reference Intervals

Unless otherwise specified, Reference Intervals are for adults (18 years of age and older) on serum or plasma samples.

The Harmonised Reference Intervals are for adoption by laboratories using methods which are traceable to The Joint Committee for Traceability in Laboratory Medicine (JCTLM)-listed reference materials, methods and services (except bicarbonate where no references are listed).

LN-RCPA is the College-endorsed preferred LOINC code from the RCPA SPIA Chemical Pathology Terminology Reference Set.

The RCPA is the leading organisation representing Pathologists and Senior Scientists in Australasia.

Its mission is to train and support pathologists and senior scientists and to improve the use of pathology testing to achieve better healthcare.
