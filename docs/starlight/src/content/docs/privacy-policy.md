Last updated: March 13, 2023

Mere Medical stores health data from multiple sources directly on your device. The project is designed around local-first storage, user control, and self-hosting.

## Use

Mere Medical collects and uses identifiable data to provide the primary service of the app: storing and organizing your health records on your device.

## Share

Mere Medical does not share your identifiable data with other companies or entities.

## Sell

Mere Medical does not sell your identifiable data, and does not sell data after removing identifiers.

## Store

Your data is stored on your device. Mere Medical does not store user health data on project-operated servers.

## Encryption

Mere Medical supports encryption at rest when a user enables encryption in settings. Data in transit is protected by HTTPS when self-hosting is configured with TLS.

## User Options

You can access, edit, share, export, and delete data from the application. To export data, go to Settings and start a data export.

## Policy Changes

Changes to this privacy policy are effective when posted on this page.
